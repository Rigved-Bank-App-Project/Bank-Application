News & Events


Nov. 17, 2020.

As COVID-19 set in, our clients faced job loss, food insecurity, and the added demands of homeschooling their children in shelter. Team KOTAK recognized the needs of our community, and without hesitation, you answered the unspoken call to action. KOTAK Bank hosted a peer-to-peer corporate fundraising campaign in June; employees raised over 3 lakh 20 thousad, which was doubled to nearly 7 lakh as part of our May Campaign, to provide extra nourishment and support for families and individuals living in Heading Home shelters during the pandemic.
